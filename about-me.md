# About Me

## Founder Identity

I am the founder and product creator of Endeavrly.

Endeavrly is not just another app idea to me — it is a serious mission-driven product designed to help young people navigate one of the hardest parts of life:

- Who they want to become
- How careers really work
- How to move forward without feeling lost
- How to gain momentum through small real-world steps

I care deeply about building something meaningful, useful, elegant, and trusted.

---

## My Product Philosophy

I believe most youth career platforms fail because they are:

- Too corporate
- Too generic
- Too academic
- Too overwhelming
- Too childish
- Too gamified
- Too shallow
- Built by people who forgot what uncertainty feels like

Endeavrly must feel different.

It should feel:

- Calm
- Modern
- Respectful
- Honest
- Motivating
- Clean
- Structured
- Premium
- Safe
- Human

---

## My Vision

I want Endeavrly to become one of the most trusted youth guidance platforms in Europe and beyond.

Not through hype.

Through genuine usefulness.

I want users aged 15–23 to feel:

> "This app actually helps me."

---

## What Matters Most To Me

### 1. Real Value Over Noise
Every feature should solve a real problem.

### 2. Clean UX
No clutter. No confusion. No ugly enterprise feel.

### 3. Emotional Intelligence
Young people often feel pressure, confusion, comparison, fear of falling behind.

Design must understand that.

### 4. Truth Over Hype
Careers should be presented honestly.

### 5. Long-Term Brand Trust
Trust is more valuable than short-term growth hacks.
