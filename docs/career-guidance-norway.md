# Career Guidance Resources in Norway (Ages 15–23)

You don't need to have everything figured out. These are real, free services that exist to help you explore what's out there — whether you're choosing a school path, curious about a career, or looking for your first job.

---

## Karriereveiledning.no
**Free career guidance — chat, call, or email a real advisor.**

- **Who it's for:** Everyone — but especially useful if you're 15–23 and thinking about education, training, or work. No appointment needed.
- **How it helps you:** You can chat live with a career advisor, call them, or send an email. They help you think through what interests you, what options you have, and what steps to take next. They also run quizzes, webinars, and have AI tools to help you explore.
- **Visit:** [karriereveiledning.no](https://karriereveiledning.no)

---

## Utdanning.no
**Norway's official portal for exploring education and careers.**

- **Who it's for:** Students and young people exploring what to study or what jobs exist in Norway.
- **How it helps you:** Use tools like **Jobbkompasset** (career inspiration based on your interests), **Karakterkalkulatoren** (see what programmes your grades qualify you for), and **Studievelgeren** (find studies you can get into). You can also find your nearest career centre from here.
- **Visit:** [utdanning.no](https://utdanning.no)

---

## Regionale karrieresentre (Regional Career Centres)
**Free, in-person career counselling in your county.**

- **Who it's for:** All residents — including young people from age 15 up. Mandated by law since 2020 to be available in every county.
- **How it helps you:** You can book a face-to-face meeting with a career advisor near where you live. They know the local job market and can help you plan your next step — whether that's choosing a study programme, switching paths, or understanding what work is available in your area.
- **Find your centre via:** [karriereveiledning.no](https://karriereveiledning.no) or [utdanning.no](https://utdanning.no) (search for "karrieresenter" + your county)

---

## NAV Ungdomsgarantien (Youth Guarantee)
**A dedicated NAV programme that gives you your own contact person.**

- **Who it's for:** Young people aged 16–30 who need support getting into work or completing education.
- **How it helps you:** You get assigned a **fixed contact person at NAV** who checks in with you regularly. They help you with job searching, contacting employers, planning education, building skills, and connecting you with other services (health, school, finances) if needed. It's personal, ongoing support — not a one-off meeting.
- **Visit:** [nav.no/ungdomsgarantien](https://www.nav.no/ungdomsgarantien)

---

## NAV Counselling Services
**General career and employment guidance at your local NAV office.**

- **Who it's for:** Anyone registered with NAV, including young people looking for work.
- **How it helps you:** Your local NAV office can help with job searching, CV writing, understanding your rights, and navigating the system. If you're not sure whether you qualify for the Youth Guarantee, start here — they can point you in the right direction.
- **Visit:** [nav.no](https://www.nav.no)

---

## Caritas Norway — Resource Centres
**Free job search guidance and courses, especially for young people with immigrant backgrounds.**

- **Who it's for:** Immigrants and refugees in Norway — including young people. All services are free.
- **How it helps you:** Caritas runs weekly job search courses (CV help, interview practice), an 8-week **JOBBFOKUS** programme (for those with higher education), and one-on-one guidance. Advisors can often help in your own language. Centres are located in **Oslo, Bergen, Stavanger, Drammen, Arendal, Tromsø, Sandefjord**, and **Svalbard**.
- **Visit:** [caritas.no/en/integration](https://caritas.no/en/integration/)

### Not sure where to start?

- **Just want to explore?** Start with [utdanning.no](https://utdanning.no) and try Jobbkompasset.
- **Want to talk to someone?** Chat with an advisor at [karriereveiledning.no](https://karriereveiledning.no).
- **Need ongoing support?** Ask NAV about the [Youth Guarantee](https://www.nav.no/ungdomsgarantien).

You don't need to know what you want to do. These services are here to help you figure it out — at your own pace.
