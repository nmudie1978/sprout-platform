# Product — Endeavrly

## Product Overview

Endeavrly is a youth-first career exploration and growth platform designed for people aged 15–23.

It helps young people understand careers, explore realistic pathways, build confidence, and move forward with clarity.

Endeavrly is not a traditional job board, personality test app, or entertainment-first platform.

It is a structured, trusted environment built to reduce confusion about the future.

---

## Core Problem We Solve

Many young people feel pressure to choose a future without truly understanding:

- What jobs are actually like
- How careers are entered
- Which paths fit their interests or strengths
- What realistic next steps look like
- How to progress without having everything figured out

Most available solutions are fragmented, shallow, outdated, or overly transactional.

Endeavrly solves this with calm, structured guidance.

## Core Product Pillars

## 1. Explore Careers

Users can browse careers in a modern, low-pressure way.

Features may include:

- Career library
- Filters
- Search
- Salary insights
- Growth outlook
- Work style indicators
- Day-in-the-life content
- Compare careers

Goal:

> Help users discover options they may never have considered.

---

## 2. My Journey

The emotional and strategic core of the product.

A structured flow that helps users move deeper into clarity.

### Discover

Light introduction to a career:

- What it is
- What the work involves
- Typical lifestyle
- Salary / demand
- Why people choose it

### Understand

Deeper reality layer:

- Entry paths
- Education routes
- Skills needed
- Challenges
- Real-world expectations
- Tools of the trade
- Long-term outlook

### Clarity

Action layer:

- Personal roadmap
- Suggested next steps
- School / grade alignment
- Milestones by age or stage
- Confidence-building progress actions

Goal:

> Turn interest into direction.

---

## 3. Career Radar

A smart preference engine that helps users explore based on what matters to them.

Possible filters:

- High earning
- Fast growing
- Work-life balance
- Low entry barrier
- Hands-on careers
- Academic pathways
- Creative careers
- People-focused roles
- Global / travel roles

Goal:

> Help users discover fit without boxing them in.

---

## 4. Real Stories

One of the strongest differentiators.

Real people explain:

- How they got into their role
- Mistakes they made
- What surprised them
- What the work feels like
- What they enjoy
- What is difficult
- Advice to younger selves

Examples:

- Radiologist
- Electrician
- Project Manager
- Nurse
- Designer
- Entrepreneur
- Psychologist

Goal:

> Replace mystery with human reality.

---

## 5. Growth & Momentum

Small actions that build progress.

Examples:

- Improve CV
- Learn a skill
- Research a path
- Contact mentor
- Complete reflection
- Compare careers
- Save opportunities

Goal:

> Make progress feel achievable.
